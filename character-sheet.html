Welcome to character-sheet
A personal life dashboard. You are looking at Example Steve's data. Set up your own sheet below, or import existing data if you already have it.
1
Download instructions.md. Claude needs this file to understand the data format.
2
Create a Claude Project. Go to claude.ai, click "Projects" in the left sidebar, then "New project".
3
Add instructions.md to the project. Inside your new project, click "Add content" then "Add files" and upload the file you just downloaded. It stays attached to every chat automatically.
4
Start a chat and just start talking. Use the starters in the Journal tab if you want a prompt to begin with.
5
At the end of your session, ask Claude: "Generate my data.json" and copy what it gives you.
6
Import your data. Click the ↑ button at the bottom-right of this page, paste the JSON, and click Import.
You have unsaved local edits. Export ↓ and replace your data.json in Claude to keep them.
