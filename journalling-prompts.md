# Character Sheet — Journalling Prompts

Optional prompts for going deeper. Upload this file to your Claude project alongside `instructions.md` and Claude will draw on these in guided mode.

No need to work through these in order — use them when a session feels flat or you want to go somewhere specific.

---

## Getting oriented

- What's actually on your mind right now — not what you think you should be thinking about?
- What happened recently that you haven't fully processed yet?
- Where are you putting most of your energy? Is that where you'd choose to put it?
- What's the one thing you keep not doing, and what do you think that's about?
- What would you most like to avoid talking about today?

---

## Main quest and side quests

- If everything external stayed the same and you could only change one thing, what would it be?
- What does "done" actually look like — specifically, not theoretically?
- What would you be doing if you weren't afraid of it not working?
- Which of your current goals is genuinely yours, and which did you inherit?
- What's the next smallest possible step — the one you could do today?
- What are you tolerating that you've stopped even noticing?
- Where are you waiting for permission that you could give yourself?

---

## The four elements

**Air — awareness**
- What has your inner voice been saying most often lately? Is it accurate?
- When do you feel most in your head? What's usually happening just before?
- What are you avoiding noticing?
- When did you last feel genuinely present?
- What do you keep misreading in yourself or others that keeps costing you?
- What pattern is so familiar you've stopped questioning it?

**Fire — drive and direction**
- What made you feel most alive recently? What drained you most?
- Are you doing things because you want to, or because you're supposed to?
- What would you work on if no one would ever know?
- Where is your energy going that isn't giving anything back?
- When you're overextending, what are you trying to prove — and to whom?
- What would you stop doing tomorrow if you had full permission?

**Water — aliveness and desire**
- What do you actually enjoy — not what you think you should?
- When do you lose track of time?
- What have you been denying yourself, and why?
- What does rest actually feel like for you — and when did you last have it?
- What are you chasing that you think will finally make you feel okay?
- What would you do more of if you stopped feeling guilty about wanting it?

**Earth — beliefs and groundedness**
- What story are you telling yourself about this situation? Is it true?
- Where are you being harder on yourself than the evidence warrants?
- What would you tell a close friend in your exact position?
- What belief would you have to give up to move forward?
- What do you believe about yourself that you've never seriously questioned?
- Finish this honestly: "People like me don't..."
- Where did that belief come from — and does the source still deserve your trust?

---

## Values and needs

- What matters most to you based on how you actually spend your time — not in theory?
- Where do you feel most like yourself?
- What do you consistently sacrifice, and is it worth it?
- What need are you currently meeting in ways that cost you more than they give?
- When you're at your most difficult, what are you usually not getting enough of?
- What are you mistaking for a want that is actually a need?
- What would you need to feel genuinely okay — not successful, just okay?

---

## Relationships and allies

- Who do you feel safest with — where you don't have to manage yourself?
- Who drains you most consistently? What's the pattern?
- Where do you perform rather than just exist?
- What relationship needs something from you that you haven't given it?
- Who would you be more yourself around if you stopped worrying about their reaction?
- What do you find it hardest to ask for, and who do you find it hardest to ask?

---

## Enemies and patterns

- What pattern keeps showing up across different areas of your life?
- What do you do when you feel threatened or uncertain? What does that protect?
- When do you procrastinate — what are you actually avoiding?
- What's the story you tell yourself to justify staying stuck?
- If your most self-destructive habit had a job, what would it be protecting you from?
- What behaviour of yours would you be embarrassed if someone named out loud?
- What do you keep doing even though you know it doesn't help?

---

## History and origin

- What did you learn early on about what you had to do to be safe, loved, or valued?
- What role did you play in your family growing up — and are you still playing it?
- What did the most influential person in your early life model about how to handle difficulty?
- What do you wish someone had told you when you were younger?
- What are you still trying to prove to someone who may not be watching anymore?
- Where did your relationship with your own worth come from?

---

## Beliefs

- What would you need to believe to take the next step?
- What evidence are you ignoring because it contradicts the story?
- What belief, if it turned out to be false, would most change how you live?
- What would the most honest version of you say about the story you're currently telling?

---

## Perspective and inversion

- What would make 85-year-old you as miserable and regretful as possible? What does that tell you?
- What objects or people are you mistaking for your actual needs? What do they represent?
- How could you make this feel more like play and less like work?
- What would you do with this challenge if you optimised for curiosity instead of performance?
- What systems or structures would remove the need for willpower here?
- What would the most energised version of you do right now?

---

## Wins and achievements

- What have you done recently that deserves more credit than you gave it?
- When did you act against your own fear or resistance?
- What would past-you be impressed by about where you are now?
- What belief or identity has shifted in you permanently — where there's a real before and after?
- What have you let go of that you used to grip tightly?

---

## End of session

- What's the one thing from this conversation you most want to remember?
- What's the one thing you're going to do differently before the next session?
- What are you still not saying?
- What question are you hoping I don't ask?
